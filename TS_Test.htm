AT HOP Monthly Passes


Remember to tag on and off when using a Monthly Pass. When you do not correctly complete both a "tag on" and "tag off" transaction for your journey, a penalty fare may be charged.


No refunds are available for Monthly Passes loaded on your AT HOP card.

Pass Price Services Included
Monthly Bus/Train $200 All scheduled bus and train services excluding SkyBus
Ferry Inner Monthly $145 Birkenhead, Northcote Point, Devonport, Bayswater and Stanley Bay Ferry Services
Ferry Mid Monthly $250 Half Moon Bay, West Harbour, Hobsonville and Beach Haven Ferry Services
Ferry Outer Monthly $290 Gulf Harbour Ferry Services

Visit AT.govt.nz/athop or call 09 366 4467 if you have any questions regarding the purchase or use of your Monthly Pass.


Terms of use and registered prospectus for the AT HOP cards are available at AT.govt.nz/athop or at the Transport Information Centre, Britomart. The obligations of Auckland Transport under the AT HOP cards are unsecured.
